About Me.

Upon teaching myself HTML and CSS, I immediately fell in love with coding. The place where my right brain and left brain worked together in unison, creating appealing artwork from logically pieced code. I knew I had to expand my knowledge and dive deeper into this awesome development world.

As soon I had recieved my Communciations degree in May 2017, I went to pursue my next goal, completing a full immersion coding program. I left my Web Development job in October 2017 to attend LearningFuze in Irvine, CA and started to learn Javascript. Twelve 80-hour weeks later, I had successfully created web apps using jQuery, ES6, React, Redux, Node.js, and learned many other technologies. Now, I am ready to take these acquired skills and start creating as a Front End Developer.
